Anthony's Hobbies

Socializing through online gaming

In his free time, Anthony enjoys video games, especially role-playing games (RPGs). Fantasy and mythological concepts has been an interest of his, and being given the ownership to create a unique character that is that is all your own, can be very empowering. He tends to hyperfocus while playing, but his current top games include Pokémon (Shield and Unite), Final Fantasy XIV and Overwatch 2. In the world of RPGs, there are livestreaming platforms such as Twitch, where users can come together as an online community and watch others stream videos of themselves playing games and users can comment. Anthony enjoys streaming videos on Twitch, showing support particularly for LGBTQ+ streamers of color. When he is able to,, he tries attend conventions that are hosted for fans of video games. It is a good way to meet other fans and see the costumes that people create. It is also a fun way to be allowed the opportunity to meet some of the people involved in creating your favorite games.

Digital art is an outlet for creativity

He also has an interest in exploring digital art. He enjoys being creative, with a love for aesthetics. At one of his job positions, he was given the opportunity to explore Canva, which he attributes to being a great source for his creativity. Canva is a graphic design tool that utilizes a drag-and-drop interface that tries to simplify the overall process of digital designing. He appreciated seeing how the application grew in the last 6 years, as it has allowed him the resources to create wonderful visuals and interactive animations that are really engaging. He feels that Canva has helped him to be able to share education and awareness in ways he never thought he was capable of doing.

Find your inner diva

Anthony is a fan of drag stemming from his love and appreciation for RuPaul’s Drag Race. This is a reality television competition hosted by RuPaul that given hopeful Drag Queens the opportunity to be “America’s next drag superstar.” RuPaul has been a huge proponent in bringing drag into mainstream society through his music and growing popularity and interest in the reality show competition. This sparked his interest in incorporating his love for drag by regularly attending drag shows with friends in his free time. It is a way for people of all backgrounds to just come together and have fun and not take things so seriously. It’s great when the queens engage with the audience, it brings everyone together. While he doesn’t participate in the art of Drag, quoted as saying “I love makeup, but it just isn’t my forte,” being able to experience drag performances in person has been a one-of-a-kind experience.
